Guide and Tips for tourist

  1. Pack only what you need.
    Don't bring too much stuff.
  2. Learn a few local words.
    Saying "hello" and "thank you" is helpful.
  3. Be ready for changes.
    Sometimes plans don't go as expected.
  4. Keep your things safe.
    Make copies of your passport and hide money.
  5. Bring snacks and water.
    You might get hungry or thirsty while traveling.
